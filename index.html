Places Must Visit

Kodaikannal

In kodaikanal I saw the the majestic , wonderfull, and mind blowing natural and man-made visiting areas they are guna cave, pillar rock , pine forest, Green valley and lots more . I saw the beauty there. That place was very cold and foggy . It was full of coconut trees . Then we started to climb the mountain.

Tip: And don't forget to bring your Jackets or else you will become an Ice lolly (kulfi).



Mysore

I saw the Mysore palace and it was chilly out there, it was so fascinating that I did not want leave that place. The great Mysore palace is the place that attracted me the most. It was so crowed and full of people.

Tip : If you want to go there and don't want to be in the crowd then go there after Dusshera so you will enjoy the view of the palace.
